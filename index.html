Our Story

REPOTRANS was founded with a mission to revolutionize financial management for MFIs, SACCOs, and community banking institutions across Kenya. We identified a gap in the market for affordable, comprehensive, and user-friendly management systems tailored to the unique needs of African financial institutions.

Our team brings together expertise in financial technology, banking operations, and software development. We've worked with institutions of all sizes and understand the challenges they face in managing loans, savings, and client relationships.

Today, we're proud to serve leading financial institutions across Kenya, helping them streamline operations, make data-driven decisions, and provide better service to their clients.
